What We Do

R K Automation & Technologies specializes in providing integrated solutions in the field of Electrical Installations, Pump Automation, Centralized Water Logging Systems, and Call Centre Operations. Our systems are tailored to meet the requirements of clients in government, infrastructure, and industrial sectors.

  • Electrical Installation: SITC of HT & LT Panels, Transformers, LED fittings, wiring and rewiring, etc.
  • Pump Automation: PLC-HMI based automation of water distribution and monitoring systems with water level sensors.
  • Centralized Water Logging Systems: Commanding and Monitoring Modules for smart water drainage and control.
  • Complaint Call Centre: CRM-integrated, QR-enabled systems for efficient complaint tracking and resolution.
  • Surveillance & Access Systems: CCTV, Access Control, Boom Barrier, Token Display Systems, and related integration services.
